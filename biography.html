Luke Bassett

My story

I've been playing with computers since I was a kid, I knew back then that I wanted to have a career that included computers. I originally wanted to be an engineer and so I learnt PC components and built my own PC.

In high school I did a computer science course where I learnt just how many different careers in tech. After finishing that course, I wanted to be a developer and so at the time I learnt basic python and java initially. A few years later I did a computing HND to narrow down my career path and during that course I found that Front-End development was what I really enjoyed. Since completing my HND I have been self-teaching front end development skills like HTML, CSS and JavaScript in the hopes of getting my first job in the tech industry as a junior developer.

As a person

I believe that I am a good team player. I like working with people that know more than I do so I can watch and understand what they do and then implement that into my own work. I also like working with the more creative individuals as well as I think when creating applications design is as important as anything else.
